Naruto was born as the son of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, and Kushina Uzumaki. However, after the Nine-Tails attack on Konoha, he grew up as an orphan, facing rejection and loneliness due to the beast sealed inside him.

Despite being ridiculed and underestimated, Naruto never gave up on his dream of becoming Hokage. Through hard work and determination, he trained under powerful mentors like Kakashi Hatake, Jiraiya, and others.

Naruto’s journey was shaped by his friendships, especially with Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno. His rivalry with Sasuke pushed him to become stronger, while bonds with Iruka, Kakashi, and Jiraiya provided him with the guidance he needed.
